FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services.

Our MOQ varies by product type. For standard products, MOQ starts at 500 pieces per design. For custom orders with branding, MOQ typically starts at 1,000 pieces. Contact us for specific requirements - we're flexible for the right partnership.

Standard orders typically take 2-3 weeks for production. Custom orders with special materials or branding may take 3-4 weeks. Express production is available for urgent orders with additional fees. We always provide clear timelines upfront.

Yes! We offer free samples for standard products (shipping cost applies). For custom orders, we provide paid samples that are deducted from your final order. Sample lead time is typically 5-7 days. We want you to be 100% confident before placing orders.

We accept T/T, L/C, PayPal, and Western Union. For first-time orders, we typically require 30% deposit with balance paid before shipping. Regular clients may qualify for open payment terms. Currency options include USD, EUR, and GBP.

Absolutely! We ship to 120+ countries worldwide. We work with major freight forwarders (DHL, FedEx, UPS) and offer sea freight for large orders. All shipments include tracking and insurance. Shipping costs are calculated based on volume and destination.

Yes! We offer comprehensive customization including logo printing (screen, offset, digital), embroidery, embossing, debossing, and metal hardware. Our design team will work with you to perfect your artwork and ensure the best production method for your vision.
